About

KanjiSheets© is a powerful tracing sheet creation tool. It lets you build custom tracing sheets quickly and easily. Just search by kanji definitions and click away at the results to add the kanji. Or you can type the kanji directly into the selection grid. Once you've designed your custom practice sheet, all you have to do is print (CTRL+P). Or save as PDF if you want a digital copy!

Data sources

KANJIDIC2

KANJIDIC2 is a database of kanji that includes readings, meanings and much more information such as stroke counts and lookup numbers for various kanji books.

KanjiVG

Stroke order data for kanji come from the excellent KanjiVG project, by Ulrich Apel and several contributers.
